Today's song is The Logical Song by Supertramp.
Life has a way of taking "A miracle, oh it was beautiful, magical" to "Oh clinical, oh intellectual, cynical".
Most refer to that as "Monday", and that is sad.



Come on. The saxophone solo makes this song, let alone the powerful lyrics.
Funny how a song from the 70's could blend whimsical elements with a sincere reflection of responsibility.
Breakfast in America is an excellent album and "Goodbye Stranger" also holds a special place in my heart.
